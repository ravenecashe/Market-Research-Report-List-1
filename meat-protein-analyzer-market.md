<p><p>The Meat Protein Analyzer market encompasses various technologies used to assess protein content in meat products. DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology offers precise measurements of protein levels, fat, and lean mass, utilizing X-ray radiation for detailed composition analysis. NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy) technology, on the other hand, employs light wavelengths to evaluate meat composition quickly and non-destructively. Both technologies are essential in quality control, ensuring nutritional standards, and enhancing product development in the meat industry.</p></p>
