<p><ul><li>VRLA Batteries</li><li>Flooded Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler lead-acid battery market comprises mainly two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, offering better safety and performance in varied conditions, making them popular for modern scooters and motorcycles. In contrast, flooded batteries are traditional, utilizing liquid electrolyte, and require regular maintenance. While flooded batteries are often more cost-effective, VRLA batteries are increasingly favored for their efficiency, compactness, and prolonged lifespan, driving their growth in the market.</p></p>
