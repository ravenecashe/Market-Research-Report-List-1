<p><p>The Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) market primarily features two types: Darrieus and Savonius turbines. Darrieus turbines, known for their eggbeater shape, are efficient at converting wind energy into electricity, suitable for high-wind areas. In contrast, Savonius turbines, resembling a scooped design, excel in low-wind environments, offering simplicity and durability. Both types provide advantages such as low noise and the ability to capture wind from any direction, driving growth in renewable energy adoption and diversified applications in urban and rural settings.</p></p>
