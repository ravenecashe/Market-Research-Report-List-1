<p><p>The skylight window market can be categorized into three main types: manual skylights, powered skylights, and securing skylights. Manual skylights are operated by hand, allowing users to open and close them as needed for ventilation and natural light. Powered skylights use electric mechanisms for convenient operation, often incorporating sensors for automatic adjustment based on weather conditions. Securing skylights are designed with enhanced security features to prevent unauthorized access while providing natural illumination, combining functionality with safety.</p></p>
