Rock wool, derived from volcanic rock, provides excellent fire resistance and sound absorption. Polystyrene, available in both expanded (EPS) and extruded (XPS) forms, offers effective insulation and moisture resistance. Together, these materials enhance noise control and improve energy efficiency in construction.</p></p>
