<p><ul><li>Microwave Sensors</li><li>Infrared Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic door sensors enhance accessibility and convenience in various settings. Microwave sensors use electromagnetic waves to detect motion, enabling doors to open when someone approaches, making them effective in high-traffic areas. Infrared sensors, on the other hand, rely on heat signatures to identify movement, activating doors when individuals are nearby. Both sensor types offer distinct advantages; microwave sensors are better for larger spaces while infrared sensors are often used in quieter environments. Together, they support seamless entry and exit experiences.</p></p>
