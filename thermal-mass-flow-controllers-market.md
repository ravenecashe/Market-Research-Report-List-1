<p><p>The thermal mass flow controllers market is segmented into contact and non-contact types based on their operational mechanisms. Contact type controllers utilize thermal sensors that directly engage with the fluid, allowing for precise flow measurement and control. Non-contact type controllers, on the other hand, employ techniques such as ultrasonic or laser-based sensing to measure flow without physical interaction, reducing wear and contamination risks. Both types address various industrial applications, enhancing process efficiency and accuracy in fluid handling.</p></p>
