<p><strong>The Calcium Carbonate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC)</li><li>Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calcium carbonate market is primarily divided into two types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is produced by mechanically grinding limestone or marble, resulting in a coarse powder used in various applications like construction, paints, and plastics. In contrast, PCC is synthesized through a chemical process, offering high purity and finer particle sizes, making it ideal for specialized uses in pharmaceuticals, food additives, and cosmetics. Both types serve diverse industries, emphasizing their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
