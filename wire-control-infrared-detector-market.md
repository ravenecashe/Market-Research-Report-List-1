<p><p>The Wire Control Infrared Detector market is subdivided into two primary types: PC (Photoconductive) and PV (Photovoltaic) detectors. PC detectors operate by changing their electrical conductivity in response to infrared radiation, making them highly sensitive and suitable for precise applications. In contrast, PV detectors generate a voltage when exposed to infrared light, offering an efficient and robust solution for various environments. Both types serve crucial roles in security systems, automation, and industrial monitoring applications, enhancing detection capabilities across diverse sectors.</p></p>
