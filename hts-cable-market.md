<p><p>The HTS cable market is primarily categorized into several types based on the materials used. YBCO cables, made from yttrium barium copper oxide, offer high performance at liquid nitrogen temperature. Bi-2212 cables utilize bismuth strontium calcium copper oxide, suitable for medium temperature applications with better mechanical properties. Bi-2223 cables are another variation of bismuth-based superconductors, known for their higher critical current density. Additionally, the "Others" category includes emerging materials and technologies that contribute to the evolving landscape of superconducting cables.</p></p>
