<p><strong>The Atomic Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>Non-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomic battery market is divided into two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion. Thermal conversion atomic batteries generate electricity by converting heat from radioactive decay into usable energy, often using thermoelectric materials. In contrast, non-thermal conversion atomic batteries directly harness the emitted radiation or charged particles, typically utilizing advanced technologies such as betavoltaic cells. Both types are utilized in various applications, including space exploration, medical devices, and remote sensors, due to their long lifespan and reliability.</p></p>
