<p><strong>The Galvanized Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-galvanizing Wire</li><li>Hot-dip galvanized Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized wire market primarily consists of two types: electro-galvanizing wire and hot-dip galvanized wire. Electro-galvanizing wire undergoes a process where a thin layer of zinc is applied through electrolysis, providing moderate corrosion resistance and a smooth finish, ideal for lighter applications. In contrast, hot-dip galvanized wire is immersed in molten zinc, creating a thicker, more robust coating that offers superior corrosion resistance, making it suitable for heavy-duty tasks like construction, agriculture, and fencing.</p></p>
