In contrast, anti-coagulant drugs reduce the risk of blood clots, which can lead to strokes in patients with atrial fibrillation. Together, these drugs play a crucial role in the comprehensive management of this common cardiac condition, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
