<p><p>The GMO testing market comprises various technologies, notably Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) and Immunoassay technologies. PCR technology amplifies specific DNA sequences, allowing for precise detection of genetic modifications in organisms. It is known for its sensitivity and specificity. Immunoassay technology, on the other hand, utilizes antibodies to identify the presence of proteins associated with GMOs. Both methods are essential for regulatory compliance, food safety, and research, ensuring that products meet labeling and safety standards for consumers and producers alike.</p></p>
