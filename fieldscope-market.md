<p><p>Fieldscope market types can be categorized into two primary designs: Straight Body Fieldscope and Angled Body Fieldscope. The Straight Body Fieldscope is characterized by a linear shape, providing a traditional viewing experience and typically favored for its simplicity and ease of use. Both types serve distinct user preferences and applications in outdoor activities such as birdwatching and nature observation.</p></p>
