<p><strong>The Aluminium Casting Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Molding</li><li>High Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Low Pressure Die Casting</li><li>Gravity Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminium casting products market includes several techniques: </p><p>1. **Permanent Molding** involves reusable molds, offering high precision and surface finish for medium to high production volumes. </p><p>2. **High Pressure Die Casting** employs molten aluminium injected at high pressure for intricate shapes, ideal for large-scale production. </p><p>3. **Low Pressure Die Casting** uses controlled gas pressure, producing lightweight, strong parts with minimal defects, suitable for complex designs. </p><p>4. **Gravity Die Casting** relies on gravity to fill molds, producing durable components with good surface quality, predominantly for lower-volume applications.</p></p>
