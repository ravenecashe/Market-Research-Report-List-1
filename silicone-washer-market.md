<p><p>The silicone washer market includes two primary types: flat washers and spring washers. Flat washers provide a smooth surface that distributes load across the surface, preventing damage and ensuring a secure fit in various applications. Spring washers, on the other hand, are designed to exert a spring force, maintaining pressure and compensating for wear or loosening over time. Both types are crucial in industries like automotive, construction, and electronics, where durability and reliability in sealing and fastening are essential.</p></p>
