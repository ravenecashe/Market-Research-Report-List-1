<p><p>The wheel lifters market is categorized into three main types: hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Hydraulic wheel lifters utilize hydraulic fluid for lifting, offering high capacity and stability, ideal for heavy vehicles. Electric wheel lifters operate using electric motors, providing convenience and ease of use, suitable for lighter applications. Pneumatic wheel lifters leverage compressed air, delivering quick lift and lowering capabilities while being lightweight. Each type serves specific needs, enhancing efficiency in vehicle maintenance and tire servicing.</p></p>
