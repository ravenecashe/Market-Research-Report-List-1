<p><strong>The Rectangular Magnetic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-Permanent Chuck</li><li>Permanent Magnet Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rectangular magnetic chuck market comprises two main types: electro-permanent chucks and permanent magnet chucks. Electro-permanent chucks utilize an electromagnetic system for holding ferromagnetic workpieces, providing strong magnetic force that can be easily controlled and switched on or off. In contrast, permanent magnet chucks rely on strong, fixed magnets to secure the workpieces without the need for electrical power, offering simplicity and reliability. Both types are widely used in machining processes, providing efficient workholding solutions.</p></p>
