<p><p>The blood pressure monitoring market is primarily divided into two types: automated BP monitors and ambulatory BP monitors. Automated BP monitors are electronic devices that provide quick and accurate readings, often used in clinical settings or for personal use at home. In contrast, ambulatory BP monitors are portable devices worn by patients over 24 hours, allowing for continuous monitoring of blood pressure throughout daily activities. This helps to capture fluctuations and provides a comprehensive understanding of an individual's blood pressure levels.</p></p>
