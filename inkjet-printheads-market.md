<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inkjet Printheads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Print Heads</li><li>Thermal Print Heads</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet printheads market is primarily categorized into three types: piezoelectric printheads, thermal printheads, and others. Piezoelectric printheads utilize electrical charges to deform a piezoelectric material, ejecting ink droplets with precision, making them ideal for a variety of inks. Thermal printheads generate heat to vaporize the ink, creating bubbles that force droplets out, commonly used in consumer printers for their efficiency. Other printhead types may include MEMS and continuous inkjet systems, catering to niche applications or specialized printing needs.</p></p>
