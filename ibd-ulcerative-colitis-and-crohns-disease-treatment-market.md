Integrin antagonists work by disrupting inflammation pathways, while corticosteroids provide rapid relief from flare-ups through immune suppression. These treatments are vital for managing symptoms, inducing remission, and improving patients' quality of life, reflecting the diverse approaches in tackling inflammatory bowel diseases.</p></p>
