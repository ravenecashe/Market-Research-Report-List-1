Non-Screen Type Films are used directly with X-ray radiation, providing high-resolution images and minimal distortion, making them suitable for applications requiring precise flaw detection. Screen Type Films utilize fluorescent screens to enhance image quality and sensitivity, allowing for quicker exposure times and better detection of imperfections in various materials. Each type serves specific needs in industrial inspections and quality control processes.</p></p>
