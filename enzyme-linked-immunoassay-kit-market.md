<p><p>The Enzyme-linked Immunoassay (ELISA) kit market comprises several types, including Sandwich ELISA, Indirect ELISA, and Competitive ELISA. Sandwich ELISA uses two antibodies to capture and detect the target antigen, offering high specificity. Indirect ELISA utilizes a primary antibody and a secondary enzyme-linked antibody, enhancing signal amplification. Competitive ELISA involves the competition between sample antigen and a labeled antigen for binding to an antibody, often used for small molecules. Other formats may include variations and specialized applications tailored to specific research needs.</p></p>
