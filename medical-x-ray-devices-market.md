<p><p>The medical X-ray devices market comprises two primary types: analog X-ray and digital X-ray systems. Analog X-ray systems utilize traditional film-based technology to capture images, producing high-quality results but requiring more time for development and a larger physical footprint. In contrast, digital X-ray systems leverage advanced sensors and computer technology to instantly capture and display images, enhancing workflow efficiency, reducing radiation exposure, and allowing for easy storage and sharing of images. This digital shift is transforming diagnostic imaging practices.</p></p>
