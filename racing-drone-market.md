<p><p>The racing drone market primarily consists of two types: ARF (Almost Ready to Fly) and RTF (Ready to Fly) racing drones. ARF racing drones require assembly and configuration by the user, allowing for customization in components like motors and controllers, appealing to enthusiasts seeking a personalized experience. In contrast, RTF racing drones come pre-assembled and are ready for immediate use, catering to beginners or those wanting a hassle-free flying experience. Both types serve different user preferences in the competitive racing scene.</p></p>
