<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Power System</li><li>Central Power System</li><li>Hybrid Power System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emergency exit lighting systems are categorized into three main types based on their power sources. **Self-Contained Power Systems** feature integrated battery packs, ensuring independent operation during power failures. **Central Power Systems** utilize centralized battery banks to supply multiple exit lights, promoting streamlined maintenance and efficiency. **Hybrid Power Systems** combine both methods, offering flexibility and reliability by integrating local batteries with centralized power, ensuring optimal performance and response during emergencies. Each type serves distinct operational needs and facility requirements.</p></p>
