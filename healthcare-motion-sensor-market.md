<p><p>The healthcare motion sensor market encompasses various types of sensors, each serving unique functions. Infrared sensors track patient movements and vital signs from a distance, ensuring non-intrusive care. Combo sensors integrate multiple functionalities, providing comprehensive data for health monitoring and rehabilitation. Together, these devices contribute to improved patient outcomes, remote monitoring, and enhanced healthcare efficiency.</p></p>
