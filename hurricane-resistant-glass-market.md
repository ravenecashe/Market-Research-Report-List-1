<p><p>The hurricane-resistant glass market comprises primarily laminated glass and insulated laminated glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more glass layers bonded with a resilient interlayer, ensuring that it remains intact during high winds and flying debris. Insulated laminated glass combines laminated glass with an insulating airspace, enhancing energy efficiency while maintaining storm protection. Both types are essential in coastal regions, providing safety against severe weather while offering aesthetic and functional benefits in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
