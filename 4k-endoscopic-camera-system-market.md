<p><p>The 4K Endoscopic Camera System market comprises two primary types: White Light Camera Systems and Fluorescence Camera Systems. </p><p>White Light Camera Systems utilize standard illumination to provide high-resolution images, aiding in clear visual examination during procedures. In contrast, Fluorescence Camera Systems leverage specialized lighting to highlight specific tissues or cellular structures, enhancing visualization of abnormalities. These systems are essential in minimally invasive surgeries, offering improved diagnostic accuracy and enabling surgeons to make more informed decisions during complex procedures.</p></p>
