<p><ul><li>Multi-stage Pump</li><li>Single-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The closed impeller pump market is categorized into multi-stage and single-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps utilize one impeller to generate flow, making them suitable for low to moderate pressure applications. In contrast, multi-stage pumps incorporate multiple impellers, allowing for high pressure and increased lift in applications requiring greater elevation or resistance. Both types serve diverse industries, with single-stage pumps commonly used in residential and light industrial settings, and multi-stage pumps often employed in process industries and water treatment facilities.</p></p>
