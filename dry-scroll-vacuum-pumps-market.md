<p><p>The Dry Scroll Vacuum Pumps market is categorized into Single-sided Scroll and Double-sided Scroll pumps. Single-sided Scroll vacuum pumps utilize one spiral to compress gases and create a vacuum, often offering efficiency and reliability for low-volume applications. In contrast, Double-sided Scroll pumps feature two spirals working in tandem, enabling higher pumping speeds and capacity, making them suitable for larger-scale operations. Both types are valued for their oil-free operation, low maintenance requirements, and ability to handle various gases.</p></p>
