<p><p>Scanning probe microscopes (SPMs) are advanced imaging tools that use a physical probe to scan surfaces at the atomic level. Key market types include Atomic Force Microscopes (AFM), which measure forces between the probe and the sample, allowing for topographical mapping; Laser Force Microscopes, which utilize laser technology for high-resolution imaging and force measurement; and Magnetic Force Microscopes (MFM), which detect magnetic forces to assess magnetic properties of materials. Together, they enable detailed material characterization across various applications.</p></p>
