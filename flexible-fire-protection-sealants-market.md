<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Fire Protection Sealants market consists of primarily two types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible and can accommodate building movement while maintaining their integrity against fire, making them ideal for joints and gaps. In contrast, intumescent sealants expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a char that seals off openings, preventing smoke and flames from spreading. Both types are essential for ensuring fire safety in construction, providing necessary protection in various applications.</p></p>
