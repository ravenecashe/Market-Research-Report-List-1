<p><strong>The GPS Receiver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Grade</li><li>Survey Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS receiver market is categorized into Differential Grade and Survey Grade segments. Differential Grade receivers enhance standard GPS accuracy by utilizing correction signals from ground stations, making them suitable for applications like agriculture and fleet management. In contrast, Survey Grade receivers provide precise positioning within centimeters, often relying on RTK (Real-Time Kinematic) technology, which is essential for detailed surveying, construction, and mapping projects. Both types serve specific user needs, balancing cost, accuracy, and application suitability.</p></p>
