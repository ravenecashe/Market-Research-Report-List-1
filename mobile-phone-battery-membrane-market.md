<p><ul><li>Porous Battery Membrane</li><li>Nonporous Battery Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone battery membrane market comprises two main types: porous and nonporous battery membranes. Porous battery membranes are characterized by their ability to allow ionic transport while retaining electrolyte, enhancing efficiency and battery life. In contrast, nonporous battery membranes prevent liquid penetration, offering excellent chemical stability and safety. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing battery performance, with porous membranes facilitating faster charging and nonporous membranes ensuring longevity and safety in mobile devices, thus driving innovation in battery technology.</p></p>
