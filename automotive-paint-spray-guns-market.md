<p><ul><li>High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP)</li><li>Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive paint spray gun market includes several types that cater to different spraying needs. High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP) guns use a high volume of air at low pressure to minimize overspray and improve finish quality, making them ideal for large surfaces. Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) guns require less air volume and pressure, offering efficiency and reduced air consumption, suitable for detailed work. Other types may include conventional and airless spray guns, each designed for specific applications and environments in automotive painting.</p></p>
