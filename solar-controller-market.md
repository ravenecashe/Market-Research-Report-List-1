<p><p>The solar controller market consists of three main types: Ordinary, PWM (Pulse Width Modulation), and MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controllers. Ordinary solar controllers provide basic functionalities for regulating solar power. PWM controllers enhance efficiency by adjusting the voltage to charge batteries optimally, while MPPT controllers maximize energy extraction from solar panels by optimizing the voltage and current output, making them ideal for larger systems. Each type serves different needs based on efficiency, complexity, and cost.</p></p>
