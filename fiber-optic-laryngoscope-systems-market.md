<p><p>The fiber optic laryngoscope systems market comprises two primary types: rigid and flexible laryngoscopes. Rigid laryngoscopes, characterized by their solid structure, provide excellent visualization and are often used in surgical settings for intubation. In contrast, flexible laryngoscopes offer enhanced maneuverability and are ideal for accessing difficult airways, making them suitable for a broader range of clinical applications. Both types utilize fiber optic technology to transmit light and images, improving the accuracy of diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in otolaryngology.</p></p>
