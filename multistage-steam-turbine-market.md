Condensing turbines maximize energy efficiency by converting steam into water, allowing for heat recovery and reuse. Back pressure turbines release steam at a pressure higher than atmospheric, making them suitable for applications requiring both power and process steam. Other types encompass extraction turbines and combined cycle units, catering to specific industrial needs and enhancing overall system efficiency in energy generation and utilization.</p></p>
