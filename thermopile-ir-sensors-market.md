<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>SMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thermopile IR Sensors market comprises two primary types: Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD) sensors. Through Hole sensors are characterized by leads that pass through the circuit board, providing robust mechanical stability, making them suitable for applications requiring durability. In contrast, SMD sensors are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards, offering advantages in compactness and higher production efficiency. Both types are utilized in various applications, including temperature measurement and motion detection.</p></p>
