<p><p>The deodorizing wound dressings market is categorized into two main types: Activated Charcoal Dressings with Silver and Activated Charcoal Dressings without Silver. The former incorporates silver ions, which possess antimicrobial properties, enhancing infection control while effectively neutralizing odors from exudates. In contrast, activated charcoal dressings without silver focus primarily on odor absorption through the charcoal's high surface area, making them suitable for managing malodor without additional antimicrobial action. Both types cater to patients with chronic wounds or post-surgical sites seeking odor management.</p></p>
