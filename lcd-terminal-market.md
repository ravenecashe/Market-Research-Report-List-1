<p><p>The LCD terminal market comprises various connection types, each facilitating video transmission. VGA terminals offer analog connections, primarily for older devices. DVI terminals support digital displays and are common in PCs. HDMI terminals provide high-definition digital audio and video, popular in consumer electronics. DisplayPort terminals enable higher resolutions and refresh rates, often used in professional settings. Thunderbolt terminals support high-speed data transfer and video output, ideal for creative professionals. USB-C terminals combine versatility, allowing video, data, and power through a single connection.</p></p>
