<p><p>The servo power brakes market encompasses three primary types: Vacuum Servo Brakes, which use atmospheric pressure to amplify braking force; Pneumatic Servo Brakes, which rely on compressed air for enhanced braking performance, commonly found in heavy vehicles; and Hydraulic Servo Brakes, which utilize hydraulic fluid to provide precise and powerful braking action. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of responsiveness, efficiency, and application suitability, contributing to their widespread adoption in various vehicles and industrial equipment.</p></p>
