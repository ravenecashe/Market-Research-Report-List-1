<p><p>Polyurethane sealants for the waterproof market are categorized into two main types: single-component and multi-component sealants. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them user-friendly and ideal for quick applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing two or more components before application, allowing for greater customization in terms of performance attributes, such as flexibility and durability. Both types offer excellent adhesion and weather resistance for various waterproofing needs.</p></p>
