<p><ul><li>Outdoor Compressor</li><li>Indoor Air-handling Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ductless mini-split heat pump market is segmented into two main types: outdoor compressors and indoor air-handling units. Outdoor compressors house the compressor and condenser, responsible for heat exchange and efficient operation in various climates. Indoor air-handling units, installed inside the living space, distribute conditioned air while allowing for individual temperature control in different rooms. This system provides flexibility, energy efficiency, and improved comfort, making it popular for residential and commercial applications, especially in spaces lacking ductwork.</p></p>
