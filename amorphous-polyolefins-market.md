<p><p>Amorphous Polyolefins (APOs) are a class of non-crystalline, thermoplastic polymers derived from olefins such as ethylene and propylene. Additionally, the growing trend of lightweight and sustainable materials in the automotive industry is boosting the demand for APOs.</p><p>Emerging trends in the market include the development of bio-based amorphous polyolefins to meet sustainability goals and regulatory requirements. Asia-Pacific is expected to dominate the market due to rapid industrialization and urbanization, while North America and Europe are witnessing steady growth driven by innovation and environmental regulations.
