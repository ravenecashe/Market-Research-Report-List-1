<p><p>Mixed reality headsets can be categorized into three main market types: tethered, untethered, and others. Tethered headsets connect to external devices like PCs, offering high processing power and graphics quality for immersive experiences. Untethered headsets operate independently, using built-in processors for mobility and convenience, making them suitable for casual users. The "others" category includes specialized or hybrid devices that blend features from both categories, catering to niche applications such as enterprise training or enhanced gaming experiences.</p></p>
