<p><p>Magnet rings are circular magnetic components used in various applications. In the **radial market**, magnet rings are designed with magnetization along the radius, making them ideal for applications requiring magnetic fields perpendicular to the axis, such as sensors or motors. In the **axial market**, magnetization occurs along the axis, creating fields parallel to it, suitable for applications like magnetic couplings or encoders. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, offering tailored magnetic solutions for diverse technological and mechanical systems.</p></p>
