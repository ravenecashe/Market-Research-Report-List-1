<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Filter Housings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side-entry Type</li><li>Top-entry Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel filter housings are essential for various filtration applications, categorized mainly into side-entry and top-entry types. Side-entry filter housings allow easy access to replace filters without disrupting the flow, making them suitable for systems with limited space. In contrast, top-entry filter housings facilitate straightforward maintenance and inspection, as filters can be accessed from the top, ideal for larger systems. Both types offer durability and corrosion resistance, catering to industries ranging from water treatment to food processing.</p></p>
