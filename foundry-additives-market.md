<p><p>The Foundry Additives market comprises three main types: organic, inorganic, and hybrid additives. Organic additives are derived from natural sources and enhance properties like strength and fluidity in foundry processes. Inorganic additives, typically mineral-based, improve the thermal stability and mechanical properties of molds and cores. Hybrid additives combine features of both organic and inorganic materials, providing a versatile solution that optimizes performance. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing foundry operations, ensuring high-quality casting results.</p></p>
