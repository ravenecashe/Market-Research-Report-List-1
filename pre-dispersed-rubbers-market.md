<p><p>The pre-dispersed rubbers market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is known for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it suitable for automotive and roofing applications. SBR (Styrene-Butadiene Rubber) is widely used in tires due to its balance of performance and cost. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) offers high oil and fuel resistance, ideal for gaskets and seals. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) provides flexibility and adhesive properties, often used in packaging and consumer goods.</p></p>
