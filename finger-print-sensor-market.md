<p><p>The fingerprint sensor market is primarily categorized into four types: capacitive sensors, ultrasonic sensors, RF sensors, and others. Capacitive sensors use electrical capacitance to capture the unique fingerprint pattern, offering quick and accurate recognition. Ultrasonic sensors employ sound waves to penetrate the skin, providing high-resolution images in various conditions. RF sensors utilize radio frequency to read fingerprints, often found in secure applications. Other market types may include optical sensors and thermal sensors, each catering to specific use cases and security needs.</p></p>
