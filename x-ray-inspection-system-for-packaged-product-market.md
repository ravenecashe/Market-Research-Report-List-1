<p><ul><li>Digital Radiography (DR) Type</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Inspection System for packaged products encompasses two main market types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR utilizes digital sensors to capture images, providing high-resolution, real-time analysis of packaged goods, ideal for detecting surface and subsurface defects. CT, on the other hand, employs a series of X-ray images to create cross-sectional views, allowing for detailed volumetric analysis. Both techniques enhance product safety and quality control by identifying contaminants and structural anomalies in packaging.</p></p>
