<p><p>The non-phthalate plasticizer market is primarily categorized into four types: adipates, trimellitates, epoxies, and benzoates. Adipates are known for providing flexibility and durability in various applications, particularly in films and coatings. Trimellitates offer high-temperature stability, making them suitable for automotive and industrial uses. Epoxies are valued for their excellent adhesion and chemical resistance, while benzoates are often employed in food packaging due to their low volatility and safety profile. Together, these types cater to diverse industries prioritizing health and environmental safety.</p></p>
