<p><strong>The Thermoelectric Module Assemblies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooling</li><li>Liquid-Cooling</li><li>Direct contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoelectric module assemblies are classified based on cooling methods: air-cooling, liquid-cooling, and direct contact. </p><p>Air-cooling systems utilize fans or natural convection to dissipate heat, making them simple and cost-effective for small-scale applications. Liquid-cooling involves circulating a coolant to absorb and transport heat, offering higher thermal efficiency for larger or more demanding systems. Direct contact cooling employs materials that facilitate heat transfer through direct physical contact, providing optimal thermal management in compact spaces where maximum efficiency is required.</p></p>
