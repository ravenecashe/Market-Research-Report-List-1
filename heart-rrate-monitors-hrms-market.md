<p><ul><li>Chest-strap HRMs</li><li>Wrist-based HRMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart rate monitors (HRMs) are devices that track heart rate during physical activity to aid fitness and health management. They primarily come in two types: chest-strap HRMs and wrist-based HRMs. Chest-strap HRMs are worn around the chest and provide accurate real-time heart rate data via electrodes, making them suitable for intense exercise. Wrist-based HRMs, typically integrated into fitness trackers and smartwatches, offer convenience and versatility, monitoring heart rate through optical sensors, making them popular for casual users.</p></p>
