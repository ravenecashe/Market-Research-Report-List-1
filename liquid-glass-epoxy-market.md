<p><strong>The Liquid Glass Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Glass Epoxy market is categorized into two main types: solventborne and waterborne. Solventborne epoxy systems contain organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and chemical resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications. However, they can emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Conversely, waterborne epoxies utilize water as a solvent, significantly reducing VOC emissions and promoting a more environmentally friendly profile. They are ideal for applications requiring lower toxicity, such as residential projects, while still providing good durability and performance.</p></p>
