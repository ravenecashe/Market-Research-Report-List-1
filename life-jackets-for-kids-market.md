<p><p>The kids' life jacket market is primarily divided into two types: neoprene and nylon. In contrast, nylon life jackets are lightweight, quick-drying, and often more affordable, making them suitable for warmer climates and casual use. Both types prioritize safety and buoyancy, catering to diverse preferences and environmental conditions, ensuring children stay secure during water activities.</p></p>
