<p><p>The VOC rotor market comprises two primary types: zeolite and activated carbon. Zeolite rotors utilize crystalline, porous structures to adsorb volatile organic compounds (VOCs), offering high efficiency in humidity and temperature variations. Activated carbon rotors, on the other hand, leverage the high surface area of carbon to capture a wide range of VOCs, making them effective for various applications. Both types enhance air quality and support environmental regulations, catering to industries focused on reducing VOC emissions and improving sustainability.</p></p>
