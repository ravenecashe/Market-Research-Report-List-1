<p><p>The non-fused switch disconnector market includes two primary types: rotary and knife-blade switches. Rotary switch disconnectors feature a rotating mechanism for operation, allowing for smooth on-off transitions and are commonly used in various industrial applications. Knife-blade disconnectors consist of a blade that is manually operated to cut or establish electrical connections, typically found in high-voltage environments. Both types ensure safe isolation of electrical circuits, providing essential protection and maintenance functionality in electrical systems.</p></p>
